Scarlet Witch

About Scarlet Witch

Wanda Maximoff, also known as the Scarlet Witch, is a Marvel Superhero, and former Avenger in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. Born in 1989, she grew up in the country of Sokovia with her fraternal twin brother Pietro. Wanda has the ability to harness Chaos Magic, but her abilities were later amplified while undergoing HYDRA experiementation using Loki's Scepter. This also caused her twin brother to develop super speed. Scarlet Witch is known to be the most powerful sorceress in the MCU, she nearely single handedly defeated the titan Thanos, until he called in an airstrike to save him. Wanda has faced many obstacles and many adversaries, and that has shaped her growth, and development into becoming the Scarlet Witch.

Wanda's Family and Friends

Wanda has a twin brother named Pietro, who takes up the alias Quicksilver, and a partner named Vision who she eventually marries, and has twin sons with. Wanda also befriends Captain America and Hawkeye, who help her on her journey in becoming an Avenger. Her family and friends have helped her throughout her journey in the Marvel Cinematic Universe.

  • Pietro Maximoff: Also known as Quicksilver, Pietro is Wanda's twin brother. He fought alongside his sister Wanda and the Avengers in the battle of Sokovia in Avengers:Age of Ultron, but eventually lost his life protecting Hawkeye who was shielding a child, after Ultron hijacked the Avengers Quinjet and began shooting at civilians.
  • Steve Rogers: Better known as Captain America, recruited Wanda and several others to join the Avengers. Due to events that occured in Sokovia, and later in Lagos, government oversight decided to step in, and have all nations, including the United States, sign a contracted called the Sovokia Accords, in order to regulate superheroes, and subject the Avengers' actions to the approval of a United Nations panel. Captain America refused to sign. Unsure of signing the accords, and afraid for her safety, Wanda was later taken to a facility, where she was held and guarded by Vision, but then rescued by Hawkeye, who was sent by Captain America. After the clash of the Avengers, Wanda and others were captured and imprisoned, but later rescued by Captain America himself.
  • Clint Barton: Also known as Hawkeye, is an Avenger responsible for giving Wanda the strength to become an Avenger. During the battle of Sokovia, Hawkeye saves Wanda, and hides her in a building after being overwhelmed by all the attacks from Ultrons sentries. Once in the building, Wanda begins to experince a panic attack which prevents her from continuing to fight. Hawkeye sees this, and reassures her that she can stay where she is, and he will send her brother for her, or she can be an Avenger, and fight with the rest. Proving that Hawkeyes words were effective, Wanda decides to step out and fight off Ultrons sentries, thus solidifying her role as an Avenger.
