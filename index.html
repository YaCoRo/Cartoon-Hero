Scarlet Witch

About Scarlet Witch

Wanda Maximoff, also known as the Scarlet Witch, is a Marvel Superhero, and former Avenger in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. She has a twin brother, named Pietro, and when first introduced, in Avengers: Age of Ultron, she and her twin seem to be working with HYDRA, but end up working with Ultron to fight against the Avengers. However, while having a conversation with Ultron Wanda discovers Ultrons actual plan to destroy earth, which causes Wanda and Pietro to go against Ultron, and align with the Avengers to take down Ultron.
Wanda is known to be the most powerful sorceress in the MCU, but that came at a cost. Wanda has faced many obstacles and adversaries, that have shaped her growth, and transformation into becoming the Scarlet Witch.
